WELCOME TO OUR BUSINESS

Heavenly Bitez is a burgeoning cookie business established in 2022, dedicated to satisfying the sweet cravings of cookie enthusiasts in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. Operating as a vendor within the esteemed Lotus Kota Bharu, our strategic choice of location aims to optimize our presence and cater to the diverse clientele that frequents the mall. Our delectable cookies come in four distinct flavors - Premium Mocha, Red Velvet, Salted Caramel, and Banana Chocolate, each carefully crafted to ensure an unparalleled indulgence in every bite.

Selecting a mall as our primary operational hub is a well-thought-out decision that aligns with our vision of Heavenly Bitez as a prominent brand. Lotus, Kota Bharu, being one of the region's premier shopping destinations, enjoys a steady influx of shoppers from diverse backgrounds. This strategic placement grants us invaluable exposure to a broad audience, including tourists, locals, and especially the vibrant youth demographic that comprises teenagers and students.

Our target market primarily revolves around teenagers and students, who are integral to the bustling atmosphere of a mall. Cookies, being a versatile treat, serve as not just a delightful indulgence but also as a source of comfort and energy for the younger population, making them an ideal study companion during rigorous academic pursuits. Additionally, Heavenly Bitez aims to foster a strong sense of community by becoming the go-to destination for social gatherings and moments of relaxation amidst the busy mall atmosphere.

Heavenly Bitez proudly presents a diverse array of cookie flavors meticulously crafted to captivate the taste buds of our discerning customers. Our Premium Mocha cookie boasts an exquisite blend of rich coffee notes and velvety chocolate, while the Red Velvet variant oozes sophistication with its luscious cocoa-infused base. For those seeking a balance of sweet and savory, the Salted Caramel cookie delivers a harmonious combination of caramelized sweetness and a hint of saltiness. Lastly, the Banana Chocolate cookie offers a delightful fusion of ripe banana goodness and indulgent chocolate.

Heavenly Bitez is not merely a cookie business it is an experience that embodies passion, craftsmanship, and the celebration of simple pleasures. By strategically positioning ourselves within Lotus Kota Bharu, we aim to cater to a diverse clientele, with a special focus on the dynamic and energetic teenage and student population. Our thoughtfully curated selection of four unique flavors promises to tantalize taste buds and leave an indelible mark on the hearts of our valued customers. At Heavenly Bitez, we believe that every bite of our cookies will transport you to a state of pure bliss, making your visit truly memorable and heavenly.
